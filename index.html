About Me

My name is Nicole. I created this website because we've adopted all of our dogs from rescue groups. They've been wonderful companions full of love. Many of my friends and family have also adopted their pets or worked with rescue groups to give these animals a second chance at life. Thank you to everyone who works in rescue. Your efforts are truly appreciated.

About this Website

This website highlights only a small number of resucue groups. All the animals in the pictures were adopted/rescued/fostered by my friends and family. I live in the USA so most of the resources are based in my country. Another SheCoder created a website about volunteering with animal shelters. The international links are from her site. She created a wonderful website. Please click the link to view it.
